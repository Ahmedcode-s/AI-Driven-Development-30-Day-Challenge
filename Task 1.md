## Reflection Activity
### 1. What does AI-Driven Development mean to you?
AI-Driven Development ka matlab hai ke hum software development mein AI ki madad le kar kaam karte hain, jaise ek saathi ki tarah.
Yani code sirf khud nahi likhte balkay AI ki madad se likhwate hain, masail solve karwate hain, naye ideas lete hain, aur apni productivity ko barhate hain.
Is ka asal maqsad yeh hai ke insaan sirf coding par waqt zaya na kare, balkay planning, reasoning, architecture aur behtar solutions par focus kare.
AI repetitive kaam sambhalta hai, jabke insaan strategic faislay karta hai.
